Futeum

Explore Together

Futeum allows hands-on experiences with hundreds of natural phenomena and technology. At countless experiment stations, visitors can experience natural phenomena first-hand and with all of their senses. "Come to grips" – in both senses of the word – with the world.

We at Futeum are committed to making an inclusive space where science free and accessible to all.

Everything in Futeum can be touched, played and experimented with: hands-on! Learning through play in a science centre by experiencing natural phenomena directly, through art that moves us, through the challenges we create in the labs and exhibitions, through dialogues with family and friends or by building your own machines in the inventor's workshop.

Futeum offers variety and inspiration for several days, both in good and bad weather, and arouses curiosity and enthusiasm for science and technology.
