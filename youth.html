Youth

at Futeum

Come join us at the Futeum where we're committed to making science accessible to all.

Over the course of human history, science has developed from our early understanding of fire, wind, water, and earth to exploring everything from galaxies far away to the very building blocks of life itself. From the inner workings of the human body to the mysteries of the universe, our exhibits offer a hands-on, interactive experience that will spark your curiosity and inspire your imagination.

Whether you're a budding scientist or just curious about the world around you, there's something here for everyone to discover and enjoy. Come and see for yourself the wonders of science!

The aim of Futeum

The aim of Futeum is to create an inclusive space where everyone can experience the wonders of our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continue the exploration of the world around us. We believe science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact with and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibits - they only come to life at night when everyone's gone home).

A community

The youth community at Futeum is vibrant and active, with many young people and teenagers coming to the museum to learn more about science and technology. Our interactive exhibits and activities are designed to appeal to young people, and we have a dedicated team of staff who are passionate about engaging and inspiring the next generation of scientists and technologists. In addition to our regular exhibits, we also offer special programs and events for young people, such as workshops, field trips, and educational games and activities. This allows young people to explore the world of science in a fun and engaging way, and to meet and connect with other young people who share their interests. We are committed to fostering a love of science in the youth community, and we hope to inspire the next generation of scientific explorers and innovators.
