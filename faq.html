FAQ

Can I book a tour for a group?

Yes, we love to have groups visiting Futem! If you are over 15 people, please contact us first by email info@futeum.com or phone 0176 278 4485

Is your entry truly free?

Yes, our entry is truly free! We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to learn more about science and technology, and that's why we offer free admission to our museum.

How many people visit Futeum?

At Futeum, we have a large and diverse audience, with many people of all ages and backgrounds visiting our museum to learn more about science and technology. We don't have an exact number of visitors, as our attendance varies from day to day, but we can say that we have a dedicated and enthusiastic community of visitors who come to Futeum to explore the wonders of science.

Can I donate to Futeum?

Yes, you can donate to Futeum! We are a nonprofit organization, and we rely on the generosity of our supporters to help us continue our mission of making science accessible to everyone. Your donation will help us to create new exhibits and activities, to provide educational programs and resources, and to support our dedicated team of staff and volunteers. If you would like to make a donation to Futeum. We are grateful for any support that you can provide, and we thank you for your generosity. Your donation will help us to inspire and educate the next generation of scientific explorers and innovators.

Am I allowed to take photos/videos?

Yes, you are allowed to take photos and videos at Futeum! We encourage our visitors to share their experiences at the museum with their friends and family, and to capture the excitement and wonder of science on camera. However, we ask that you please be respectful of other visitors and of the exhibits, and that you follow our photo and video guidelines. For example, we ask that you do not use flash photography, and that you do not block the view of other visitors or interfere with the operation of the exhibits. Additionally, we ask that you please credit Futeum when sharing your photos and videos online. We hope that you enjoy taking photos and videos at Futeum, and we can't wait to see what you capture!

How can I get a job at Futeum?

If you are interested in working at Futeum, we would love to hear from you! We are always looking for passionate and dedicated individuals who are interested in joining our team. To apply for a job at Futeum, you can visit our website and look for current job openings on the "Careers" page. You can also send your resume and a cover letter to careers@futeum.com, and we will keep your information on file for future job opportunities.
We review all job applications carefully, and we will contact you if we think that you would be a good fit for one of our open positions. We look forward to receiving your application and learning more about your interest in joining our team at Futeum.

How do I book an event?

If you are interested in booking an event at Futeum, we would be happy to help you! We offer a variety of event spaces and services that are perfect for corporate events, social gatherings, educational programs, and more. To book an event at Futeum, you can visit our contact page and write "Book an Event" as the subject. Once you have submitted the form, one of our event coordinators will contact you to discuss your event in more detail and to help you plan a successful and memorable experience at Futeum. We look forward to helping you plan your event at Futeum!

Do you have a cancellation policy?

Yes, we have a cancellation policy for events booked at Futeum. If you need to cancel your event, please notify us as soon as possible so that we can make the necessary arrangements. Our cancellation policy is as follows:
- If you cancel your event more than 30 days before the scheduled date, you will receive a full refund of any deposit or payment that you have made.
- If you cancel your event between 14 and 30 days before the scheduled date, you will receive a 50% refund of any deposit or payment that you have made. If you cancel your event less than 14 days before the scheduled date, you will not receive a refund.
Please note that our cancellation policy may be subject to change, and that it may vary depending on the specific details of your event. For more information, please contact our event coordinators.
We appreciate your understanding and cooperation with our cancellation policy.
